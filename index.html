Introduction - M G M Shareeff

I'm M G M Shareeff, a professional with expertise in engineering, entrepreneurship, and business & health coaching. My journey began with a solid foundation in engineering, which led me to build and manage successful ventures such as Z IT Service, providing after-sales service to brands like Lenovo and Dell.

Passionate about helping others, I dedicate much of my time to business and health coaching. I aim to empower aspiring entrepreneurs to unlock their potential, which is why I wrote my book, "Rapid Business Accelerator: Unlock the Secret of Entrepreneurial Success".

I'm also an active member of Junior Chamber International (JCI) and serve as Executive Director for ZP. In this role, I lead community initiatives to foster development and strengthen JCI's impact.

My approach to coaching focuses on balancing professional success with personal well-being. I believe in driving business growth while promoting a healthy lifestyle, ensuring sustainable success for my clients.

As an engineer, entrepreneur, author, and coach, I strive to innovate, lead, and serve. My work continues to inspire individuals to achieve new heights in both their personal and professional lives.
